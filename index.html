Rules

Welcome to Manechat! Keep it family friendly and SFW. Breaking the rules will result in being muted, kicked, or banned.

  1. Be pony-like and kind to other members.
    1. We are an inclusive, LGBT+ supportive server. No Nazis allowed. Discriminatory or bigoted language & behavior will not be tolerated.
    2. Follow common courtesy in voice chat. Keep it SFW, do not disturb in-progress games with off-topic chatter, and properly calibrate your microphone.
    3. Users who consistently create negative experiences in the server will be removed, even if no singular event would be considered severe.
    4. No trolling, impersonation, or similar behavior.
    5. When sharing artwork made by someone else, you must provide a source. Examples of acceptable sources include: a link to the art or artist, a Booru link or at least the artist's name.
  2. This is a SFW server. Offensive, sexual, political, and religious content are not allowed.
    1. Username, nickname, pfp, user banner, status, bio and even the external emojis/stickers you use are included in this rule.
    2. Your username/nickname must be easy to ping. It must have a few English-common character but the rest is your choice.
  3. This server is focused on meaningful and valuable conversation. Please keep your contributions relevant and easily readable.
    1. Do not spam, shitpost or emptypost.
    2. Avoid memes and reaction gifs. These will be removed at the mods discretion.
    3. This is not a roleplay server.
    4. Please do not interrupt ongoing conversations with images, videos, or links which are not related to the current topic. Either take your post to a lower-traffic channel like #tailchat or wait until the conversation ends.
  4. Links to other Discord servers, patreons, gofundmes and similar are not allowed.
    1. Links to streams, channels, and other self-promotion should be posted only once per 24 hours.
    2. Advertising commissions, YCHs, and other art related services in exchange for money should be kept to #open-commissions and no more often than once every 7 days.
    3. Contact a princess if you receive unsolicited and/or unrequested DMs from someone in our server.
  5. Please avoid spoiling new releases for other users.
    1. MLP Content that airs ahead of time is considered spoilers, even if it comes from an official source. Keep all current MLP spoilers in #g5-general.
    2. New non-MLP content should be discussed in #nonmlp-media-news and #nonmlp-spoilers.
    3. Do not carelessly spoil important plot points of any media in general, regardless of its age.
  6. We are not a support group. If you need help, please seek out the help of a professional in your area.

Do not enforce the rules or try to regulate behaviour, that is exclusively the Princesses' responsibility. If you think or feel a rule is being breached, or you have concerns, contact a Princess without worry.

All Princess decisions are final.

  • Princesses have full discretion to remove content or users deemed unpleasant or unwelcomed by community members.
  • Princesses have full discretion to change channel topics.

Respect Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

Abusing or exploiting bugs in any bot or Discord itself is a bannable offense.

If you'd like to appeal a ban, you can fill out this form and we'll get back to you.
